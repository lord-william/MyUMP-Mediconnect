Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Go to your dashboard and click "Book Appointment". Choose your preferred date and time, then fill in your symptoms or reason for visit.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment time. Contact support or use the appointment management system.
What should I do in case of emergency?
For medical emergencies, call our emergency line immediately at +27 800 123 456 or contact local emergency services at 10177.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.
